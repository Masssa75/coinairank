My Recommendation

Option A (Icon next to name) provides better visibility and immediate awareness of verification status. The shield/warning icons are intuitive and placing them next to the token name creates a strong association between the token identity and its verification status.

The compact tooltip provides just enough information without overwhelming the user, while the icon clearly signals whether this is a safe token (green shield), needs investigation (orange info), or is potentially dangerous (red warning).
